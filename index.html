  • Ce sa fac

    • 1. Use a timer to charge your airsoft gun batteries.

    • 2. Airsoft gun parts are all made to run at a specific speed. Only use your airsoft gun with fully charged batteries. If your airsoft gun is wearing down, take the battery out and charge it. Stop shooting immediately.

    • 3. If this is your first time, turn your hop up completely off and then adjust it slowly. Turning the hop up all the way up results in the airsoft gun jamming and unable to fire.

  • Ce sa nu fac

    • 1. Airsoft guns are replicas of real firearms. They are not built for prolonged periods of shooting. Like a real firearm, airsoft guns overheat and damaged is caused. Keep your shooting intervals between 4-6 second bursts. This will prevent any complications.

    • 2. Never charge a battery that has become warm or hot. Do no continue to charge it.

    • 3. When you are finished using your airsoft gun, never leave the batteries in there.

FAQ - Intrebari puse frecvent

IS AIRSOFT SAFE?Overall, airsoft is an enjoyable and safe sport, as there are strict rules in place which ensure players are aware of and able to avoid potential risks. The most significant risk of injury is the possibility of being shot in a vulnerable part of the body, such as an eye. However, full face masks are available and usually mandatory which will protect the full face and neck. In reality, as long as it is enjoyed responsibly, airsoft is no more dangerous than other team sports.
DO I NEED PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT TO PLAY AIRSOFT?There are some basic pieces of protective equipment which are recommended. The minimum requirement is a good pair of glasses or goggles which are resistant to impact. However, many players also wear face masks, which are combined with eyewear for full face, head and eye protection. In addition, protective airsoft vests, guards and boots for airsoft are also popular options.
CAN CHILDREN PLAY AIRSOFT?Although it is an offence for a person younger than 18 to purchase an airsoft gun, being gifted a gun which is used to play airsoft is not. Generally speaking, most airsoft fields will have a minimum age of between 12 and 14, although some will accept younger children.
